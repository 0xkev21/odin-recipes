Tater Tot and Bacon Breakfast Casserole

Description

A Tater Tot and Bacon Breakfast Casserole is a delightful morning dish that combines the comforting flavors of crispy bacon, golden tater tots, and creamy eggs. It's a simple yet hearty breakfast option that's perfect for feeding a hungry crowd or enjoying a cozy morning with loved ones. In this casserole, crispy tater tots and savory bacon bits are mixed with fluffy scrambled eggs, cheese, and a blend of seasonings, then baked to golden perfection. The result is a satisfying and indulgent breakfast casserole that's sure to please everyone at the breakfast table.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bacon, diced
  • 1 (32 ounce) package frozen bite-size potato nuggets (such as Tater Tots)
  • 12 large eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, 5 to 8 minutes. Drain bacon on paper towels.
  3. Spread bacon into the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish to cover. Spread potato nuggets over the bacon.
  4. Beat eggs, milk, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; pour over the layer of potato nuggets.
  5. Top with cheddar cheese and bake in the preheated oven until hot in the center, about 1 hour.
  6. Serve hot. Enjoy!
